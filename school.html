My College Experience

As mentioned before I am pursuing my degree AS degree in Entrepreneurial Studies. I can say it has been a Journey for the past 4 years, as I headed into this college stuff not knowing where to start. I came into El Camino thinking of becoming a nurse. Overtime, things had changed, and I worked on my current degree and take my chances of becoming a Realtor. I started taking class that I would never see myself taking and they genuienly helped me understand what I was pursuing and I definetly enjoyed learning things I never learned before.

How College Shapes My Goals?

College has helped me build more confidence, discipline, and a strong work ethic. As I did not enjoy too much of the courses for becoming a nurse being that it challenged me a lot mentally. However, ever since pursuing my new major, it showed me how much my Counselors, Professors, and School overall was willing to help me pursue this next big thing for me. I learned a lot about business and how to run it and the deep dark sides of it and how to overcome it which is something I really enjoyed.

Life After College

After earning my degree, I plan to get a Realtor License in California and Nevada with as this is where I plan to live within the next few months. I will get the license with this Realtor Company called Keller Williams. Once I gain my license, I want to own my own brokerage business and plan to help other upcoming entrepreneurs like me reach their goals. My education has inspired me to focus not just on success, but on helping others succeed as well.

What I Enjoy Most

One of my favorite things about school is collaborating with classmates on group projects, it helped me overcome my fear of being in groups as I am anti-social/introvert. I also enjoyed learning and taking in any new information that will benefit me in the long run. I’ve also learned to balance school, work, and personal growth is something I’ll carry with me beyond college.
